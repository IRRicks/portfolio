My Design Process





I approach design decisions using the agile method shown in the double-diamond design method. This method allows me to use both divergent (broad, all-encompassing) thinking, along with convergent (goal-specific, focused) thinking. The key steps in this process are discovery, strategy, information architecture and visual design, and development and launch.
