Presenting Problem:

There has been an increase in bus routes in this particular city. Riders are getting confused by the bus arrival and departure times. One stop in particular, Washington & State, is causing a lot of problems.

The Solution:


My solution was to design a simplified navigation system, focused on displaying the map prominently in the application, as a result of the feedback I got from the users during the discovery phase. I also wanted to make sure that users were able to quickly identify when their bus would be arriving by displaying alerts with bright, contrasting colors.
