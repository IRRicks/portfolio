    • Presenting Problem:

    Current patient portal applications feature homepages that are cluttered, confusing and fail to prioritize user flows.

    The Solution:


    My solution was to streamline the homepage. This would help reduce the cognitive overload that users experience upon opening their application. I also wanted to make the appointment scheduler more intuitive, so that older users would also feel comfortable using the application on a regular basis to make their doctor appointments.


    Discovery Phase:

    Before beginning the design process, I needed to gather information about how users felt about their current patient portal applications, the way they use them, and the features they would most like to see in future applications.

    Competitive Analysis:
